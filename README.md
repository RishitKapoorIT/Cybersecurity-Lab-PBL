# Cybersecurity Lab - PBL

This repository contains the homepage for the **MUJ-Cyberange Cyberange Lab**.

## Overview
India's premier cybersecurity research lab bridging academia and industry through advanced Cyberange training, threat intelligence, and smart city security research.

MUJ-Cyberange Cyberange Lab is a state-of-the-art cybersecurity research facility focused on smart city security, critical infrastructure protection, and next-generation defense technologies.

### Key Features
* **Smart City Cybersecurity**: Protecting next-generation urban infrastructure from cyber threats.
* **Cyberange Training**: Immersive simulated environments for offensive and defensive operations.
* **Research + Industry Collaboration**: Bridging academia and industry through joint projects and mentorship.
* **Student Opportunities**: Multiple tracks for research, innovation, and professional development.
